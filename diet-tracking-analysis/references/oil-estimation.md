# Oil Estimation Reference

1g oil ≈ 9 kcal, pure fat. Fold into each dish's calories — never list oil separately.

## Visual Cue Table (per 100g cooked food)

| Visual cue | Oil/100g |
|------------|----------|
| Matte, no sheen | 3g |
| Slight gloss | 5–8g |
| Oil film, pooling at edges | 9–12g |
| Heavy pooling, glisten | 13–16g |

## High-Absorption Dishes (fixed defaults)

| Dish type | Oil/100g | Note |
|-----------|----------|------|
| 炒蛋 / scrambled egg | 10g | 蛋液吸油 |
| 干锅 / dry pot | 15g | 食材半浸油中 |

## Cooking-Method Rules

| Condition | Oil/100g |
|-----------|----------|
| 凉拌菜 (cold dressed) | 3–5g (judge by gloss of dressing) |
| Deep-fried | 0g extra (already in nutrition data) |
| Soup | Only visible floating oil; clear broth → 0g |

## Scene & Fallback Defaults

| Condition | Oil/100g |
|-----------|----------|
| 食堂 / 外卖 / 餐厅, oil unclear | 7g |
| Cooking method unknown or missing | 7g |
