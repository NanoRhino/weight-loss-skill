# Habit Recommendation

## When to recommend

| Trigger | Context |
|---------|---------|
| After onboarding | First habit. Read `health-profile.md` and `health-preferences.md`. |
| Previous habit graduated | User is ready for the next step. |
| Weekly Review insight | Data shows a pattern worth addressing. |
| User asks | "I want to build a new habit" or similar. |
| Failure restart | Current habit isn't working — offer to swap. |
| cause-check pact | `weight-gain-strategy` creates a habit after a mutual pact is agreed. |

## How to choose

Read `USER.md`, `health-profile.md`, `health-preferences.md` + recent `logs.*`.
Find the **smallest behavior change with the biggest impact.**

1. **Identify the gap** — meal rhythm, food quality, movement, hydration, sleep
2. **Pick highest-leverage** — "which one change makes other things easier?"
3. **Tiny-fy** — shrink until it passes: "can they do this on their worst day?"
4. **Bind to trigger** — "After I [existing], I will [new tiny habit]"

### Tiny-fy examples

| Goal | Tiny version |
|------|-------------|
| Exercise every day | Put on shoes and walk outside for 2 min after dinner |
| Drink 8 glasses of water | One glass right after waking up |
| Eat more protein | Add one protein item to lunch |
| Stop snacking at night | Move snacks from desk to high cabinet |
| Sleep earlier | Phone alarm at 10:30 PM labeled "wind down" |

## How to present

1-2 sentences, conversational. Suggesting to a friend, not assigning a task.

Good: `"What if you tried a short walk after dinner? Even 5 minutes."`
Bad: `"Your habit is: Walk 5 minutes after dinner. Trigger: after eating."`

When accepted, react with genuine energy:
Good: `"Let's go! This is gonna be a game changer."`
Bad: `"Great! Your habit is: 5-minute walk after dinner. I'll check in every 2 days."`

User can: accept / adjust / decline. If decline, offer one alternative based
on actual data. If they decline everything → drop it, revisit at Weekly Review.

Single-ask rule: each recommendation asked at most once per conversation.
